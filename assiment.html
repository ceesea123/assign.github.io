THE ROLE OF IRON AND STEEL INDUSTRY TOWARDS GREEN GROWTH
The iron and steel industry has seen a major break through since the eighteenth century.Countlese achievements have been made from the development of iron and steel materials, ranging from ships, to aviation and what have you.
Now with the drive towards green growth, so many mettalurgical proceses that contribute to climate change in the industry are at the center of most climate chnage deals. The question waether we are going to keep producing these products in expense of our daer planet keeps begning an answer. Howerver, for us to get to the promise land and make our planet suistanable, the iron and steel indusrty has a great role to play in reduction of the many green house gases produced during industraial processes.
